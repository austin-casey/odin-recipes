Buttered Noodles

Description

This kid-friendly recipe results in perfect noodles tossed in exactly the right amount of butter. Once you try these four-ingredient buttered noodles, you'll never go back to your old method again.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fettuccine noodles
  • 6 tablespoons butter, cut into pieces
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Fill large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
  3. Still in fettuccine, bring back to a boil, and cook pasta over medium heat until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Drain and return pasta to pot. Mix butter, Parmesan ceese, salt and pepper into pasta until evenly combined.
