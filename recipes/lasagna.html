Lasagna

Decription

This homemade lasagna is a winner every time and it only takes 30 minutes to prepare!

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Make tomato sauce
  3. Combine 1 1/2 cups mozarella cheese, 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, ricotta cheese, parsley, egg, and salt to taste
  4. Layer sacue, noodles and cheese to achieve desired result
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes
